Who We Are

Cyber World is a modern cyber security and technology learning platform founded by Shivansh Kumar. We focus on spreading cyber awareness, ethical hacking education, web development skills, and practical technical knowledge among students and beginners.

We believe that cyber security education should be simple, practical, and accessible for everyone – regardless of their background or experience level.

// 09_FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cyber World?

Cyber World is a modern cyber security and technology learning platform that provides practical education in ethical hacking, web development, networking, and digital safety – founded by Shivansh Kumar.

Who can join Cyber World?

Anyone! Students, college students, working professionals, or curious individuals. All our courses and workshops are designed for beginners and no prior experience is required.

Is this beginner friendly?

Absolutely! All our content is designed specifically for beginners. We start from the very basics and build up step by step in a clear, practical, and engaging manner.

Will a certificate be provided?

Yes! After completing workshops and courses, participants receive digital e-certificates that can be used on resumes, LinkedIn profiles, and college portfolios.

Is coding experience required?

No coding experience is required. Our programs start from absolute zero – you only need a laptop/phone, internet connection, and the willingness to learn!

Are sessions online or offline?

Primarily online through live interactive sessions. We also conduct offline workshops at schools and colleges. All sessions are recorded for reference by participants.
